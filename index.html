About Me

Hi, I am Clare Smyth MBE (born 1978) a Northern Irish chef. I am the Chef Patron of Core which opened in 2017. Previously I won the Chef of the Year award in 2013, and achieved a perfect score in the 2015 edition of the Good Food Guide.I have also appeared on television shows such as Masterchef and Saturday Kitchen.
